Braves 2021 World Series Win

The Atlanta Braves' 2021 World Series title is one of the biggest moments in franchise history. The Braves battled through the postseason and defeated the Houston Astros to win their first World Series championship since 1995, bringing a championship back to Atlanta.

Hank Aaron's historic 715th home run

Hank Aaron's 715th home run is one of the most significant moments in Atlanta Braves history. On April 8, 1974, “Hammerin' Hank” Aaron surpassed Babe Ruth to become Major League Baseball's all-time home run leader, cementing his legacy and creating a historic milestone for both the Braves and baseball.
